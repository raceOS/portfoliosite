About RaceOS

Built by Riders,
For Riders

We started RaceOS because we were tired of riding blind. Stock dashboards tell you speed and fuel — but your bike knows so much more. We built the platform to unlock it.

RaceOS pairs with your motorcycle via Bluetooth or OBD to stream live telemetry to your phone, giving you the data that pro race teams use — right on your handlebars.

  • Real-time BLE telemetry connection
  • Ride history & performance analytics
  • Custom alert thresholds & warnings
  • Works offline — no cloud dependency

Features

Everything Your Bike
Has Been Waiting For

Live Telemetry

Stream 120+ data points per second from your bike's ECU. Speed, RPM, throttle, temperature, lean angle — all live.

Core
Ride Analytics

Every ride logged and analyzed. Compare sessions, track personal bests, and identify where you're losing time or fuel.

Core
Smart Alerts

Set custom thresholds for engine temp, battery, lean angle, and more. Get warned before your bike warns you.

Core
Route Mapping

GPS-overlaid ride tracks with elevation, speed heatmaps, and corner analysis. Replay every twist and turn.

Pro
AI Diagnostics

Pattern-based engine health scoring. RaceOS flags anomalies before they become breakdowns, saving you time and money.

Pro
Hardware Bridge

Plug-and-play ESP32 module for bikes without native BLE. Supports OBD-II and custom sensor arrays.
